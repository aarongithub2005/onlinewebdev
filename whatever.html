10 Amazing Ways to Improve Your Health

Health is wealth they say. Whether you are a child or an adult entering into your old age, you need health to function properly. The world around us complicated the way we view health, but according to me it is easy to maintain your health by following simple and effective ways. So here are some of them......

1. FOOD

Food is any nutritious substance that people or animals eat or drink or that plants absorb in order to maintain life and growth So it is important to monitor the food we consume everyday because our life and growth depends on it. Many health problems in our body start from intake of toxic and proccessed foods. Maintaining a proper diet can help improve our gut health which inturn improves our overall health.

2. Excercise

Excercise is a great way to improve you health as it pumps blood to all parts and organs in our body while do it. Exercise also helps in digestion of food and also slows down ageing process. Lifting weights is also a great form of exercise as it builds muscle and makes us look fit and healthy. Atleast 1-2 hours of excercise is recommended everyday.
